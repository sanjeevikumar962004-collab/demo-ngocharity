Common Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most common questions our team receives.

Are donations made to Stackly NGO tax-exempt?

Yes, all financial donations made to Stackly NGO are eligible for tax deductions under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. A valid receipt along with our 80G registration certificate is sent directly to your registered email address right after a successful transaction.

How can I apply to become a field volunteer?

You can head over to our events page and register for any upcoming drive, or choose "Volunteering Opportunities" in the contact form above. Our volunteer coordination team will contact you to align on your skills, schedule, and interests.

Can I visit Stackly's active projects on the ground?

Absolutely! We value transparency above all. Contact us via the form above with your proposed travel dates and the specific initiatives (e.g., mobile clinics, education centers) you'd like to visit, and we'll arrange a hosted field visit.

How does Stackly ensure the transparency of funds?

We undergo strict external financial audits twice a year and publish detailed financial reports on our blog and annual impact reports. Over 94% of collected funds go directly to community resources, with only 6% used to cover essential logistics.

Our Process

How Your Donation Works

We ensure every rupee reaches those who need it most through a rigorous, transparent four-step process.

1

You Donate

Choose any cause and donate securely. 100% of your donation goes to the program — no admin deductions.

2

We Verify

Our field team verifies every beneficiary on-ground before any funds are disbursed to ensure zero fraud.

3

Aid Delivered

Resources are delivered directly — food, medicines, training materials — with real-time tracking and GPS verification.

4

You See Impact

Receive photo and video updates from the field showing exactly how your donation made a difference.
